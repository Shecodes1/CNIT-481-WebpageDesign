Dumbbell Bicep Curl

The dumbbell curl is an exercise that targets the bicep muscles on the front of your arms. Dumbbell curls allows for a greater range of motion than barbell curls. Begin the exercise by by grabbing a pair of dumbbells with an underhand grip. Lift the dumbbells from waist level to shoulder level using a hinge motion at your elbow. Elbows should stay in place while bring the weight up to your shoulder. Exercise can be performed with multiple reps and sets. It is recommended to do four sets of 8-10 reps or or until failure.
